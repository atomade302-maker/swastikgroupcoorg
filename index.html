Discover Swastiks

Where Serenity
Meets Adventure

Located in Madikeri Town, Coorg, Swastiks Balaji Homestay offers a unique combination of peaceful retreat and outdoor excitement. Wake up to the aroma of coffee blossoms, spend your day fishing by the river pond, and end your evening under a canopy of stars by a roaring bonfire.

Scenic Location

Surrounded by misty mountains and coffee estates.

Local Cuisine

Authentic local recipes prepared fresh by our chef.

Outdoor Activities

Camping, fishing, trekking, and plantation walks.

Cozy Nights

Guided campfire experiences under the night sky.

Things to Do

Experiences & Activities

Create unforgettable memories with our curated resort activities suitable for friends, couples, and family.

Roaring Bonfire Nights

Gather around the warm crackle of our community campfire, share stories under the starlit sky, and enjoy light music. Perfect Coorg night experience.

Pond Fishing & Angling

Spend a quiet, peaceful afternoon at our fishing pond. Try your hand at catching local freshwater fish. We supply the fishing rods and bait!

Traditional Local Dining

Taste the rich spices of Coorg and Malnad. We serve piping hot home-style meals, traditional delicacies, and barbecues freshly roasted to order.

Plantation & Nature Walks

Take guided strolls through our private coffee plantation. Learn how coffee is grown, spot native birds, and breathe in the crisp, unpolluted mountain air.
